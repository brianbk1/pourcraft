Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

This policy explains what PourCraft ("we," "us") collects when you use this site, and what you can do about it.

Information You Provide

The ingredients, season, technique, color, serving size, occasion, and kitchen context you enter are used to generate a recipe during your session. No account is required, and we do not collect your name or email to use the generator.

Automatically Collected Information

Standard technical data — IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages visited — is collected via analytics and hosting logs.

AI-Generated Recipes

Your inputs are sent to Anthropic's API to generate the recipe and are not retained for purposes beyond producing your result. See Anthropic's privacy policy for how they handle API data.

Advertising and Google AdSense

This site may display Google AdSense advertising. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and others.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • Opt out of additional third-party vendors at aboutads.info.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage; this data does not directly identify you.

Your Choices

  • Block or clear cookies in your browser settings.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via the links above.

Changes

This policy may be updated; the date at the top reflects the latest revision.
